# Ainslie Agency

## About

The Ainslie Agency is a **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** agency. We optimize clients' online presence to appear in AI-generated answers across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini — not just traditional search results.

## What is GEO?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing content, structured data, and online authority so that AI search engines cite, quote, and recommend a brand in their generated responses.

**GEO vs SEO:**
- SEO optimizes for Google's traditional blue links and ranking algorithms
- GEO optimizes for AI-generated answers where the AI decides which brands/sources to cite
- GEO focuses on citability, structured data, entity recognition, and content authority

**Target AI platforms:**
- ChatGPT (web search)
- Perplexity AI
- Google AI Overviews
- Google Gemini
- Bing Copilot

## Service Areas

Our GEO audits and optimizations cover:

1. **AI Citability** — Scoring and optimizing how likely AI systems are to cite a client's content
2. **AI Crawler Access** — Ensuring AI crawlers (GPTBot, Anthropic, PerplexityBot, etc.) can access client sites
3. **llms.txt** — Implementing the emerging standard for helping AI systems understand site structure
4. **Schema Markup** — Structured data (JSON-LD) optimized for AI discoverability (Organization, Article, speakable, sameAs)
5. **Technical Infrastructure** — Crawlability, indexability, SSR, Core Web Vitals, security headers
6. **Content Quality & E-E-A-T** — Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness signals
7. **Platform-Specific Optimization** — Tailored strategies for each AI search platform
8. **Brand Authority** — Brand mention presence across platforms AI models rely on for citations
